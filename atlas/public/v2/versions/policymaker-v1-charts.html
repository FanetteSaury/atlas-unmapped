👋 Welcome to Cohort Insights

For programme officers from NGOs and country offices designing youth-employment cohorts.

This dashboard surfaces the Atlas talent layer — verified, anonymised skill signals from young people in informal sectors across Ghana and Bangladesh.

Start by exploring the 10 informal-skill clusters below. Each card crosswalks cohort share, female participation, wage band, automation risk and skill-gap demand — all sourced from ILOSTAT, WDI, WBES, Frey-Osborne LMIC and WBL 2024.
